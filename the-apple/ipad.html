Our Products

iPad

The iPad is a line of tablet computers.It also one of the products designed and also announced by our forever idol Steve Jobs.
The main product line was followed by the iPad mini, iPad Air, and iPad Pro.
The user interface is built around the device's multi-touch screen, including a virtual keyboard. All iPad models include built-in Wi-Fi and cellular connectivity is available as an option.
The iPads initially ran a modified version of the iOS operating system for iPhones which was later replaced by iPadOS.
An iPad can shoot video, take photos, play music, and perform Internet functions such as web-browsing and emailing. Other functions like games, reference, GPS navigation, social networking, etc. And can be enabled by downloading and installing apps.
The first iPad had a 9.7 inch screen display and a button replacement.
